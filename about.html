About me
I first taught myself to code from a book on Python programming that I found online back in high school. As a kid who liked creating things and writing stories, knowing how to code opened up an entirely new world for me. I then enrolled in university to major in Computer Science and Physics where, through a combination of coursework and self-teaching, I became an increasingly versatile programmer. I now have experience in Python, Java, JavaScript, C# and C++. I am competent with various frameworks such as Django, Flask, IndexedDatabase, PostgreSQL, and more. I love open-source and free software, and am always looking for a cool coding project to sink my hours into. I am based in Johannesburg, South Africa.
